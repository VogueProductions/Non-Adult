Important Notice

Read every point carefully before proceeding.

This is a legally binding document under Indian law. You are providing consent on behalf of a person under 18 years of age. Providing false information is a criminal offence.

Do NOT fill this form if any of the following apply:

  • You are not the biological parent or court-appointed legal guardian
  • You have been offered money, gifts, or anything of value to sign this form
  • The minor has expressed unwillingness, fear, or hesitation
  • You have not personally read or had explained to you all the terms
  • The minor is below 6 years of age
